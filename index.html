Welcome to Elite Photo Logger

This program was written in response to Elite Dangerous not having any form of Pilot's Log functionality.

It's difficult to keep track of what you're doing in Elite, so instead of having to alt-tab out, simply hit the screenshot button (F10) to capture your screen. You can then come back later and review all of the images from your session to write you own pilot's log.
